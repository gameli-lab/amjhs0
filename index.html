About Our School

Adeiso Methodist Junior High School (JHS) is a notable educational institution located in Adeiso, within Ghana's Eastern Region. Situated along the Juaso - Nsawam Road, the school operates from Monday to Friday, opening its doors at 6:00 AM and closing at 3:00 PM.

Recognized as one of the leading and competitive schools in the Upper West Akim District, Adeiso Methodist JHS has garnered a reputation for its commitment to quality education. The school actively engages with the community through its Facebook page, where it shares updates and information.
